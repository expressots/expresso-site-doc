Support us

ExpressoTS is an MIT-licensed open source project. It's an independent project with ongoing development made possible thanks to your support.

Your support, in any form, is greatly appreciated. Together, we can make ExpressoTS the go-to framework for developers around the world.

How you can support us​

There are several ways you can support the growth and development of ExpressoTS:

  • Become a Sponsor on GitHub: Financial support helps us allocate more time and resources to the project, accelerating its growth and enabling us to bring new features and improvements faster.

  • Follow and Star the Project on GitHub
    Show your support by following our organization on GitHub and starring ⭐ the project. This helps increase our visibility and attract more contributors.

  • Join our Community on LinkedIn: Join the community on LinkedIn to stay up-to-date with the latest news, updates, and events related to ExpressoTS.

  • Join Our Discord Community: Connect with other ExpressoTS users and contributors on Discord]. Share your experiences, ask questions, and get involved in discussions about the framework.

  • Spread the Word: Share ExpressoTS with your friends and colleagues. The more people use and contribute to the project, the better it becomes!

Thank you! ❤️​

Thank you for being a part of our journey!

If you have any questions or suggestions, feel free to reach out to us on Discord. We'd love to hear from you!
