# Support us

ExpressoTS is an MIT-licensed open source project. It's an independent project with ongoing development made possible thanks to your support.

Your support, in any form, is greatly appreciated. Together, we can make Expresso<span class="span-table">TS</span> the go-to framework for developers around the world.

## How you can support us

There are several ways you can support the growth and development of ExpressoTS:

-   **[Become a Sponsor on GitHub](https://github.com/sponsors/expressots)**: Financial support helps us allocate more time and resources to the project, accelerating its growth and enabling us to bring new features and improvements faster.

-   **[Follow and Star the Project on GitHub](https://github.com/expressots/expressots)**  
    Show your support by following our organization on GitHub and starring ⭐ the project. This helps increase our visibility and attract more contributors.

-   **[Join our Community on LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/expresso-ts/?viewAsMember=true)**: Join the community on LinkedIn to stay up-to-date with the latest news, updates, and events related to ExpressoTS.

-   **[Join Our Discord Community](https://discord.com/invite/PyPJfGK)**: Connect with other ExpressoTS users and contributors on Discord]. Share your experiences, ask questions, and get involved in discussions about the framework.

-   **<span class="span-table">Spread the Word</span>**: Share ExpressoTS with your friends and colleagues. The more people use and contribute to the project, the better it becomes!

## Thank you! ❤️

Thank you for being a part of our journey!

If you have any questions or suggestions, feel free to reach out to us on **[Discord](https://discord.com/invite/PyPJfGK)**. We'd love to hear from you!
