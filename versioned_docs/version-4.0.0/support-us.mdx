# Support us

ExpressoTS is an independent, MIT-licensed open-source project. Active development is made possible by the people who use it, sponsor it, and contribute to it.

Whatever form your support takes, it matters. Together we can make Expresso<span class="span-table">TS</span> the go-to TypeScript framework for backend developers everywhere.

## Roadmap and support commitment

- **v4.0.0** ships in **May 2026** and is supported through **May 2028** (24 months of bug & security fixes).
- **v3.0.0** is in bug-and-security mode through **December 4, 2026**.
- See [Release notes → Support policy](./prologue/release.mdx#support-policy) for the full version matrix and [Roadmap](./roadmap.mdx) for the upcoming milestones.

The longer the support window, the more important your support is. Sponsorship and contributions directly fund maintainer hours that keep the supported lines patched.

## How you can support us

There are several concrete ways to help the project grow:

-   **[Become a Sponsor on GitHub](https://github.com/sponsors/expressots)**: financial support lets us allocate more time to the project, accelerating feature work and keeping the supported lines patched on schedule.

-   **[Follow and Star the Project on GitHub](https://github.com/expressots/expressots)**: visibility brings contributors. A star takes a second; it materially helps.

-   **[Join our Community on LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/expresso-ts/?viewAsMember=true)**: the place we announce releases, talks, and partnerships.

-   **[Join Our Discord Community](https://discord.com/invite/PyPJfGK)**: talk to other users, ask questions, help triage issues, and shape the roadmap.

-   **<span class="span-table">Contribute code or docs</span>**: pick anything labelled `good first issue` or `help wanted`. See [Governance](./governance.mdx) for the contributor path.

-   **<span class="span-table">Spread the word</span>**: write a blog post, give a talk, file a benchmark, ship a template. The more real-world adoption ExpressoTS gets, the better it becomes.

## Enterprise support

If your organisation runs ExpressoTS in production and needs a guaranteed support response, an extended support window beyond the 24-month baseline, or private security advisories ahead of public disclosure, reach out directly on Discord and we will schedule a conversation.

## Thank you ❤️

Thank you for being part of this journey.

If you have questions or suggestions, ping us on **[Discord](https://discord.com/invite/PyPJfGK)**. We always love hearing from people using the framework in the wild.
