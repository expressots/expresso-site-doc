# Roadmap

The roadmap is a high-level overview of where ExpressoTS has been and where it is going. It is a living document and will be updated as work lands and new priorities emerge.

Smaller features and improvements that are too narrow to plan publicly are tracked on GitHub.

## Working packages

Working packages group related tasks so they can be planned and shipped together. Each package eventually maps to a minor or major release.

> -   **Enhanced**: the area was reworked or significantly improved.
> -   **Support**: the area is supported with first-class APIs and documentation.
> -   **Non-existent**: the area is on the radar but not yet shipped.

## Timeline

The timeline is a rough estimate of when we expect to complete the work. It is not a guarantee, and the actual timeline may vary.

-   **Q1**: January – March
-   **Q2**: April – June
-   **Q3**: July – September
-   **Q4**: October – December

## How this maps to the support policy

See [Release notes → Support policy](./prologue/release.mdx#support-policy) for the source of truth. Highlights:

- **v4.0.0**: released **May 2026**, supported through **May 2028** (24 months).
- **v3.0.0**: released December 2024, support extended to **December 2026** alongside the v4 launch.
- **v2.0.0**: end-of-life since March 2025.

## How to influence the roadmap

1. **Open a discussion** at [github.com/expressots/expressots/discussions](https://github.com/expressots/expressots/discussions).
2. **File an RFC**: for anything cross-cutting (new module, new package, breaking change). See [Governance → Decision making](./governance.mdx#decision-making).
3. **Pick up a planned item**: any "Planned" row above can be claimed. Comment on the tracking issue or open one if it doesn't exist yet.
